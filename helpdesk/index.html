Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?
A: Use the password reset link on the login page or contact IT support.

Q: My email is not working. How can I fix it?
A: Check your internet connection, try logging in from another browser, or contact IT support.

Q: I need a new email account for testing.
A: Contact IT support stating that you need a new email account. State your exact reason and keep in mind you may require approval from your lead.

Q: I've lost access to my company Roblox account. What should I do?
A: Immediately contact your lead and make them aware. Do not attempt to create a new one prior to your lead's instructions.
